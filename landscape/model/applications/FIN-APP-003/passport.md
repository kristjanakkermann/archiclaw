# TM1 Planning (FIN-APP-003)

IBM TM1-based operational planning tool used for departmental budgets and workforce planning. Receives actual data from SAP ERP via nightly CSV extracts.

## Key Facts

- **IBM TM1 10.2** with TurboIntegrator ETL, on-premise
- Nightly batch CSV extract from SAP ERP
- Used by department heads for bottom-up budget submissions

## Criticality

**Tier 3** system. Non-critical outside of budgeting season. Candidate for retirement once SAP BPC covers operational planning use cases.
