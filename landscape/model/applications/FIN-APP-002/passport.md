# SAP BPC (FIN-APP-002)

Business Planning and Consolidation platform for financial planning, budgeting, and statutory consolidation. Consumes actual data from SAP ERP and provides planning models accessible via Excel.

## Key Facts

- **SAP BPC 10.1** on HANA DB, same on-premise infrastructure as SAP ERP
- Used by Finance team for annual budgeting and rolling forecasts
- Pulls actual financial data from SAP ERP via HANA Views

## Criticality

**Tier 2** system. Critical during planning cycles (Q4) and close periods, but not real-time.
